Australia, Home of the worlds largest living organism!

If you are visiting australia you need to visit the great barrier reef!
it is the largest living organism in the world! This great barrier reef is home
to many different animals. When visiting you may see inshore dolphins, dugongs whales
and maybe even sharks! this is just naming a few!

The Great Barrier Reef is composed of over 2900 individual reefs! It is
over 1400 miles long! Can you even imagine! Coral reefs have been around
for over 500 million years, but the Great Barrier reef is only 500,000 years. Its
most modern form is only 8000 years old! Thats pretty young for a coral reef!
