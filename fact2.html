Some of the worlds most unique animals!

Are you an animal lover? Well, Australia might just be the place for you! Australia is
home to some of the most interesting animals. They are most known for Kangaroos
and Koalas. Have you ever been driving down the road and seen a deer crossing sign?
Well, in australia they have these for their Kangaroos. Kangaroos keep their young (also known as joeys) in a pouch in their stomach until they are able to be out in the world.

Koalas are beautiful animals found in Australia! They can sleep for up to 19 Hours! thats
the majority of the day! Koalas can live to around 15-20 years! They usually weight 20-30
and are about 3 feet in length. There are even sanctuaries in Australia where you can hold
and hand feed Koalas. So Koala lovers, this is the stop for you!
